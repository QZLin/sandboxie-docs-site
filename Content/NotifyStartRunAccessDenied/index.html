Notify Start Run Access Denied

NotifyStartRunAccessDenied is a sandbox setting in Sandboxie Ini. It is typically specified as NotifyStartRunAccessDenied=y, and indicates that Sandboxie should issue message SBIE1308 when programs are denied from starting or running.

Usage:

   .
   .
   .
   [DefaultBox]
   NotifyStartRunAccessDenied=y

Related Sandboxie Control setting: Sandbox Settings > Restrictions > Start/Run Access Related Sandboxie Control setting: Program Settings
